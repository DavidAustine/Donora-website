
# 📘 **DOCUMENT 1: Advanced Networking & Infrastructure**

## **1. Background**

* **Demand**

  * Surge of demands to **design, evaluate, manage, maintain sophisticated network infrastructures**
  * **Big data**

    * Processing and analyzing **massive amounts of data**
    * Requires:

      * **distributed file systems**
      * **distributed databases**
      * **cloud computing platforms**
      * **Internet storage**
  * **Cloud computing**

    * Move IT operations to **Internet-connected infrastructure**
    * Causes **drastic shift in networking requirements**

* **Internet of Things (IoT)**

  * Large numbers of objects using **standard communications architectures**
  * **Billions of devices interconnected**
  * Provides:

    * **new interactions between physical world and computing**
  * Applications:

    * **healthcare, home automation, energy, farming, transportation, education**

* **Mobile devices**

  * **Indispensable part of enterprise IT infrastructure**
  * Includes **BYOD**
  * Creates **new demands on network planning and management**

* **Capacity**

  * **Gigabit data rate networks**

    * Ethernet up to **100 Gbps**
    * Wi-Fi ~ **7 Gbps**
    * **4G and 5G networks**
  * **High-speed, high-capacity servers**

    * Needed for **multimedia and data processing**

* **Complexity**

  * Requires:

    * **QoS (Quality of Service)**
    * **QoE (Quality of Experience)**
    * **flexible, manageable networking hardware**

* **Security**

  * Increasing need for **security services**

## **5. Quality of Communication**

* Successful when:

  * **message understood = intended meaning**
* Issues caused by:

  * **network factors**

### **External QoS Factors**

* **Quality of pathway**
* **Number of transformations**
* **Redirection/readdressing**
* **Network traffic**
* **Time allotted**

### **Internal QoS Factors**

* **Size of message**
* **Complexity of message**
* **Importance of message**

---

## **6. The Networking Ecosystem**

* Exists to provide **services to end users**
* User platforms:

  * **fixed (PC)**
  * **portable (laptop)**
  * **mobile (smartphone)**

### **Access Facilities**

* **DSL, cable modems**
* **Wi-Fi, WiMAX**
* **cellular networks**

### **Providers**

* **Application providers**

  * Apps run on user device
* **Application service providers**

  * Software runs on **provider platform**
  * Examples:

    * **web servers**
    * **email servers**
    * **cloud computing provider**
* **Content providers**

  * Provide **data (email, music, video)**

### **Additional Elements**

* **Data center networking**

  * Large number of interconnected servers
* **IoT / fog networking**

  * **machine-to-machine communication**
